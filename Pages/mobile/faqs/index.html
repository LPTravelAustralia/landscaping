Frequently Asked Questions

Request Service

  • Are you licensed and insured?

    Yes, we're fully licensed and insured professionals. We maintain proper coverage and credentials for all lawn care, landscaping, and snow removal services in West Michigan.

  • Do you serve areas outside Sparta?

    Yes, we serve Sparta, Rockford, Cedar Springs, Grand Rapids, Comstock Park, and Walker. Our local crews provide reliable service throughout West Michigan communities.

  • What's included in seasonal cleanup services?

    We clear leaves and debris during spring and fall transitions. Services include storm cleanup and bed redefinition to enhance curb appeal year-round.

  • Do you offer commercial snow contracts?

    Yes, we provide 24/7 commercial snow management including plowing, hauling, and salting. We offer seasonal or per-event contracts for businesses and property managers.

  • Can you install outdoor kitchens?

    Yes, we install paver patios, fire pits, seating areas, and outdoor kitchens. All designs are built for durability against Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles.

  • How do you handle drainage problems?

    We implement French drains, regrading, and runoff management solutions. Our systems protect lawns and foundations from Michigan's heavy rain and spring thaw conditions.

  • Do you use eco-friendly practices?

    Yes, we're dedicated to eco-conscious practices including sustainable material choices, environmentally safe turf treatments, and responsible waste removal for all services.

  • What's your landscape design process like?

    We create custom outdoor designs blending function, beauty, and budget. We collaborate closely with homeowners to refine concepts and materials throughout the project.

  • Can you build retaining walls?

    Yes, we build structurally sound retaining walls, walkways, and stone features. All projects are expertly graded and engineered for Sparta's varying lot elevations.

  • Do you offer year-round commercial services?

    Yes, we provide comprehensive year-round landscape, turf, and snow management for businesses. We offer bundled maintenance and enhancement options with flexible scheduling.
