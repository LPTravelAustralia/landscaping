Who We Are

Better Than Before LawnCare & Landscaping is a licensed and insured, family-owned landscaping company with deep roots in West Michigan. We founded this business on the principle that every property — from modest residential yards to commercial grounds — deserves expert care delivered with professionalism and attention to detail.

We approach every project guided by three core values: integrity in all our dealings, precision in every task we perform, and a commitment to putting customers first at every stage. From your initial inquiry through the final walkthrough, you'll experience the difference that genuine care makes.

Our team serves homeowners, property managers, HOAs, and commercial clients throughout the Sparta area and surrounding West Michigan communities.

Our Story

Better Than Before LawnCare & Landscaping was founded in the Sparta, Michigan area by a team passionate about transforming outdoor spaces and providing reliable, quality service to local homeowners and businesses.

What started as a focused lawn care operation grew steadily through word-of-mouth referrals and repeat customers who trusted us with their properties season after season. That grassroots growth reflects our commitment to delivering results that genuinely exceed expectations — not just meeting a standard, but raising it.

Today we offer a full suite of exterior services including lawn maintenance, landscape design and installation, hardscaping, tree and shrub care, and snow removal. Whether you need ongoing seasonal programs or a one-time project, our experienced team is equipped to deliver professional results that last.

We're proud to serve our local communities and invest back into the region that has supported us since day one.
