Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lawn care, landscaping, and seasonal services in Sparta and West Michigan.

Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes, Better Than Before LawnCare & Landscaping is fully licensed and insured. We carry comprehensive liability coverage and workers’ compensation to protect you and your property on every project.
Do you serve areas outside Sparta? +
Yes. We regularly serve Rockford, Cedar Springs, Grand Rapids, Comstock Park, Walker, and surrounding West Michigan communities. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific area.
What is included in seasonal cleanup services? +
Our seasonal cleanup services include leaf and debris removal, bed edging and cleanup, pruning and trimming of shrubs, lawn dethatching, and preparation for the upcoming season. Spring and fall cleanups are available as standalone services or as part of a maintenance package.
Do you offer commercial snow removal contracts? +
Yes. We offer seasonal commercial snow removal contracts for businesses, HOAs, and multi-unit properties. Contracts include plowing, salting, and ice management. Contact us in the fall to arrange contract pricing before the season begins.
Can you install outdoor kitchens? +
Yes. Our hardscaping team designs and installs custom outdoor kitchens with features such as built-in grills, countertops, storage, and fire features. We’ll work with you to create an outdoor entertainment space that fits your lifestyle and budget.
How do you handle drainage problems? +
We assess your property’s drainage issues and recommend solutions such as French drains, dry creek beds, swales, or regrading. Proper drainage is essential for protecting your lawn, landscaping, and foundation from water damage.
Do you use eco-friendly practices? +
Yes. We prioritize environmentally responsible practices including targeted fertilization programs to minimize runoff, native plant recommendations for lower water usage, organic treatment options where appropriate, and responsible disposal of all materials.
What is your landscape design process? +
We begin with a site consultation to understand your goals, budget, and vision. From there we develop a design proposal with plant selections, hardscape elements, and a project timeline. Once approved, our installation team brings the design to life with professional craftsmanship.
Can you build retaining walls? +
Yes. We construct retaining walls using natural stone, block, and other quality materials to manage slopes, erosion, and create usable level areas. All retaining wall projects include proper drainage planning to ensure longevity.
Do you offer year-round commercial services? +
Yes. We provide year-round commercial landscape maintenance programs that include lawn care, seasonal cleanups, snow removal, and landscape enhancements. Ask us about customized commercial maintenance contracts for your property.
